Dayhoff Technologies Website Solution

Biotechnology Research





BRIEF

Dayhoff Technologies offers a web-based automation platform for biotech applications. Their solution is designed to streamline processes from sequencing to reporting, enhancing sample management, automating access to high-performance computing (HPC) resources, and facilitating real-time data reporting.

The platform also supports seamless integration with existing laboratory information management systems (LIMS), ensuring smooth data flow and reducing the potential for errors. Additionally, it provides advanced analytics tools that allow users to gain deeper insights from their data, enabling faster decision-making and improving the overall efficiency of biotech research and development.




RESEARCH

User research for Dayhoff Technologies focuses on understanding biotech professionals' needs, challenges, and workflows. Insights from surveys and testing emphasize the importance of seamless LIMS integration, easy HPC resource access, and real-time data reporting. This research ensures the platform improves efficiency, enhances productivity, and simplifies complex biotech processes.




UIUX DESIGN

UI/UX design for Dayhoff Technologies emphasizes user-centered principles, creating an intuitive and efficient interface. The design focuses on seamless navigation, clear data visualization, and responsive features. By aligning with users' needs, the platform offers a smooth experience for biotech professionals, enhancing productivity and simplifying complex workflows with an aesthetically pleasing layout.
