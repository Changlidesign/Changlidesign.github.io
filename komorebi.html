Komorebi App, by CureApp®

Hospitals and Health Care





BRIEF

Komorebi App, developed by CureApp®, is a digital health tool designed to support patients with high blood pressure. CureApp® specializes in "Digital Therapeutics," a pioneering approach that uses clinically validated software as a medical device for treatment. With its strong foundation in scientific research, CureApp® has become a leader in digital therapeutic solutions.

The Komorebi App leverages cognitive behavioral therapy principles to help users establish healthier habits and make informed lifestyle choices. Through targeted guidance, it aims to empower patients in managing their blood pressure effectively, contributing to improved long-term health outcomes.




RESEARCH

User research for the Komorebi App focuses on understanding the unique needs of patients managing high blood pressure, particularly seniors. Through surveys, interviews, and usability testing with both patients and healthcare providers, researchers gather insights into users’ lifestyle challenges, digital habits, and treatment goals. This research helps the team refine the app’s functionality, ensuring it is intuitive and meets the daily needs of older adults.

By collaborating with hospitals and clinicians, Komorebi’s research tailors its features to fit clinical contexts, enabling seamless integration into medical care. The findings support Komorebi’s mission to empower users with accessible, evidence-based health guidance.




FEATURES

1. Encouraging Behavior Change: Using Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) techniques, the app guides users toward healthier habits and mindset shifts to improve long-term health.

2. Blood Pressure Tracking: Komorebi connects with Bluetooth-enabled devices to automatically record blood pressure readings, providing users with a seamless tracking experience.

3. Health Report Sharing: Users can easily share detailed health reports with their physicians, promoting informed, collaborative care.

4. Bite-Sized Educational Content: The app provides brief, digestible health tips and information, helping users stay educated and engaged in managing their health.

UI Design

UIUX DESIGN

The UI/UX design of the Komorebi App emphasizes user-friendliness and accessibility, catering to a health-focused experience for managing high blood pressure. The clean, minimalistic layout with ample whitespace and clear icons guides users through the app seamlessly, making it easy to set up and monitor their blood pressure. Large, readable fonts and straightforward instructions ensure a comfortable experience for users of all ages, particularly seniors.

Komorebi’s interface prioritizes intuitive navigation, with features like Bluetooth pairing instructions and easy-to-read health data summaries. Visual aids and progress indicators further enhance usability, allowing users to track their journey toward healthier habits effortlessly.




UI Component

The Komorebi App UI features have soft green tones create a calming experience, while toggle switches, progress indicators, and a clean navigation bar enhance usability, supporting users in their health management journey.
