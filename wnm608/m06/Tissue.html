意米(YIMI) is a kind of cultural and creative brand, it comes from Jinlin,China. YIMI refers to five different kinds of rice: rice, red beans, mung beans, black rice, and millet. YIMI is a cultural creative brand, representing the characteristics of Jilin, mainly selling products such as star films, stationery, cards, books, stationery, office supplies and so on.
