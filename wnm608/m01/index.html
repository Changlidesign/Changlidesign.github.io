    Steps

      1. Break apart and wash vegetables.
      2. Bring the water to a boil and add a little salt to the noodles.
      3. Mix the noodles and add cold water.
      4. Heat the pan with oil and beat in the eggs.
      5. Cut until golden on both sides.
      6. Roll the noodles and add the lettuce.
      7. Add a little salt and light soy sauce and lard to the bottom of the soup.
