1. Concept

1.1 Joinn Concept

Joinn

Joinn is an app that allows you to join local interest groups freely. After the user's city is automatically located, users can choose to join their favorite groups, participate in group discussions and offline activities. In addition, users can create groups, manage group members, and make new friends.

Using Joinn, users can mark your offline activities on their calendar, chat with the group members, and keep track of the group updates. After joining Joinn groups, you will find your interests, meet new people, and you won't feel lonely any more.

1.2 About our team

The Team

This is a project I worked on with my teammates during my internship. Joinn helps users join local interest groups and make new friends. We find it difficult to stick to a goal or habit on our own. For example, reading books, exercising or learning new things. So, we encourage users to join the group, and have a common interest people adhere to the goal together, achieve the goal, and move forward new friends.

7. Conclusion

Summary Takeaway

1. The power of the collective is often greater than that of the individual.
2. People like to do what they are interested in with like-minded friends.
3. When being alone, people often find it hard to stick to their goals and fail again and again, but when people join the team, we don't want to fall behind, we will finish the task under the mutual supervision of the team members.

What I learned

In the process of completing the project, the first thing I learned was the importance of teamwork and timely feedback. In this internship, we had a meeting with the leadership every day to report the progress of the project. After receiving the feedback, we will immediately carry out the intra-group discussion and task assignment. Joinn was done in this timely and orderly way. The second point is creativity. If an app of the same type stands out in the market, it needs different creative ideas and new functions. Joinn focuses on local groups, sets up online and offline events, and recommends users to participate actively and work with their members to achieve their goals.
